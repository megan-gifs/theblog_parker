ABOUT ME

Hello there, my name is Parker Leabo! I was born and raised just north of San Francisco and have lived in San Francisco for the last 3 years. I am in love with this city and I find a certain magic here I haven't found anywhere else. I am passionate about illustration, fine art, tattoo flash, art esoterica and graphic design. I currently am attending the University of San Francisco getting my B.A. in Design. In addition, I am currently a barista which allows me to connect with many different people in the city. In my free time, I discipline myself to sketching, painting and ideating every day. I have found my way to my passion for all things visual - advertisement, rebrands, fliers, fine art, tattoo flash, logo design. I believe in hard work with rigorous practice that I hope is reflected in my work.
